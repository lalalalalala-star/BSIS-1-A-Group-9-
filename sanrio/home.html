Sanrio, founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, is built on the philosophy of "Small Gift, Big Smile," spreading joy through adorable characters and thoughtful gifts. It all began with cute greeting cards, but in 1974, Hello Kitty was introduced, quickly becoming a global icon of friendship and kindness. Over the years, Sanrio has created a beloved cast of characters, including My Melody, Cinnamoroll, Kuromi, Pompompurin, and the Little Twin Stars, each bringing their own charm and personality. Today, Sanrio continues to inspire happiness worldwide through fashion, accessories, theme parks, and collaborations, proving that a little bit of cuteness can make the world a brighter place!
